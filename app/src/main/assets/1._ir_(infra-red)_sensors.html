3. IR (Infra-Red) Sensors

There are various types of IR sensor available. We will be using either 2 legged IR sensors or Pololu QTR 8A Sensor array for making Line following robots.

Generally IR sensors consists of a receiver which detects IR rays and IR an emitter which emits IR rays. There are also Analog and Digital IR Sensors. We will stick with Analog IR Sensors since they give us analog values and we have the flexibility to convert the analog value into digital but if we use digital IR sensor then we can not determine the color of the surface in which the IR ray was reflected.
