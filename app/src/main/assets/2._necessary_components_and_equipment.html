2. ESSENTIAL COMPONENTS

Things you need to have

  1. An Arduino Board
  2. The Arduino IDE
  3. Motor Driver (L293D or L298N)
  4. Two DC Motor (6V) with Tires
  5. A Plexiglass platform to assemble your robot on
  6. Or A Magician Chassis or similar Robotic Kit would work too
  7. Lithium Polymer Battery / Lithium Ion Battery / Or Any DC Power Source
  8. TCRT 5000 Analog IR sensors or Pololu QTR-8A IR Sensor array
  9. LDR or Light Dependent Resistor
  10. LEDs (Any Color)
